Nylon Flowers

Great for a gift or center peices at weddings or events!

These were very popular in the 70s! Each petal is hand crafted with care by skillfully wraping a thin peice of wire into a circle then wrap it over with nylon fabric. After the construction of the petals, they are contorted to the desired shape and arranged in a decorative fashion, resembling all kinds of flowers.
